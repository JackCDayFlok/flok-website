Hospitality Recruitment
Across Every Sector

Hospitality is diverse - and so are we.

Flok Jobs connects venues with skilled hospitality professionals across the UK, from Michelin-starred restaurants to large-scale events, casual dining, and specialist catering. Whether you need experienced chefs, professional bartenders, skilled waiters, or reliable support staff, our network of verified hospitality workers covers every sector.

Fine Dining &
Luxury Hospitality

Premium venues deserves exceptional staff. We specialise
in recruiting for:

  • Michelin-Star Restaurants - Head chefs, sous chefs, and experienced kitchen teams
  • Fine Dining & AA Rosette Venues - Skilled front-of-house professionals, Wine waiters, Head chefs, sous chefs, and experienced kitchen teams
  • Luxury Hotels & 5-Star Resorts - Hotel management, concierge, Wine waiters, Head chefs, sous chefs, and experienced kitchen teams and guest services staff
  • Private Hospitality - Private chefs for households, superyachts, and luxury chalets

Find experienced hospitality professionals who understand luxury
service standards and fine dining excellence.

Pubs, Restaurants
& Casual Dining

The heart of UK hospitality. Flok Jobs supplies staff for:

  • Gastro Pubs & Traditional Pubs - Bar staff, kitchen teams, and pub managers
  • Restaurant Chains & Independent Eateries - Servers, hosts, and kitchen porters
  • Cafés & Coffee Shops - Baristas, café assistants, and shift supervisors
  • Fast-Casual Dining - Line cooks, food prep staff, and customer service teams

Reliable, flexible hospitality staff for busy venues across the UK.

Events & Corporate
Hospitality

Large-scale events require trusted professionals. We provide staff for:

  • Weddings & Private Events - Event servers, bartenders, and catering teams
  • Festivals & Stadium Catering - High-volume food service and beverage staff
  • Corporate Events & Conferences - Professional event staff and hospitality teams
  • Exhibition & Trade Show Catering - Experienced event hospitality professionals

Vetted hospitality staff ready for any event size or venue.

Institutional
& Contract Catering

Serving communities across the UK. Our recruitment covers:

  • Healthcare Catering - Hospital kitchens, care homes, and medical facilities
  • Education Sector - School kitchens, university dining, and student services
  • Government & Public Services - Military bases, prisons, and public institutions
  • Corporate Dining - Office canteens, executive dining, and workplace catering

Specialised hospitality recruitment for institutional and contract
catering needs.

Travel & Specialist
Hospitality

Unique venues, exceptional staff. Flok Jobs recruits for:

  • Cruise Ships & Airlines - Galley staff, cabin crew hospitality, and cruise dining teams
  • Railway Catering - Train service staff and mobile hospitality teams
  • Street Food & Food Trucks - Mobile catering specialists and festival vendors
  • Film & TV Location Catering - On-location catering teams for productions

Remote & Specialist Locations:

  • Offshore Platforms & Remote Sites - Industrial catering and camp services
  • Holiday Parks & Resorts - Seasonal hospitality staff and entertainment venues
  • Community Kitchens - Social enterprise catering and community dining

Adaptable hospitality professionals for any location or environment.
