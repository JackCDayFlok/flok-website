Built by Hospitality,
for Hospitality

No agencies. No faff. Hospitality hiring, sorted.

Flok is the UK's leading platform for restaurant and hotel jobs, created by experts with over 25 years experience in Michelin-starred kitchens, luxury hotels like those in Mayfair, major UK events and private dining across Europe.

We connect verified chefs, bartenders, servers and back-of-house staff directly with restaurants, hotels, pubs, bars and catering businesses — instantly.

No agencies, no commissions, no middlemen. Flok offers fast, fair hiring solutions that empower venues and give professionals access to quality jobs across the UK.

Why Flok Exists

We've faced the chaos of last-minute staff cover falling through, chefs calling in sick mid-shift and managers scrambling during peak service.

We've navigated staff shortages, unreliable agencies and outdated hiring systems that don't match the industry's pace. Flok was built to fix that with a direct, reliable solution created by those who've worked every role in hospitality.

The Future of Hospitality Hiring

Flok is built for hospitality's unique needs, with location-based matching, skill verification, and instant booking. Venues find reliable staff quickly; professionals access flexible, high-quality work with verified employers.

We're creating the UK's largest network of hospitality talent, ready for kitchens, bars, and events when needed most.
