How does Flok work?

1. Post

Employers create a shift or position with the role, location and hourly rate.

Instantly send it to the Flok community or your favourite go-to workers, all from your phone or computer.

2. Hire Fast

Hire from verified candidates and view their profiles, experience and availability.

Prefer speed? Let Flok's smart system do the matching for you.

3. Pay Fast

Once the shift is completed, payment is sorted automatically.

Flok makes hiring and paying staff seamless, transparent and stress-free.
